What MarinLoop Does

Daily Timeline

Visual schedule of every dose — past, present, and upcoming — color-coded by status.

Push Reminders

Native push notifications that fire at the exact scheduled time, even when the app is closed.

Medication Management

Add medications by name or barcode. Track dosage, frequency, supply, and refill dates.

Health Tracking

Log vitals, journal entries, and view adherence trends over time in one place.

Care Network

Share your care plan with providers, caregivers, and emergency contacts.

AI Assistant

Optional, informational tools only. Voice commands and label extraction — opt-in, revocable.
