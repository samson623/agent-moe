Features

Everything you need to
stay on track.

Built for patients managing daily medications and caregivers who need visibility into routines.

🕓

Daily Timeline

See every scheduled dose for the day with a live adherence ring. Tap to log, navigate between days, and track your progress over time.

🔔

Push Reminders

Server-dispatched push notifications for doses and refills. Works even when the app is closed. Snooze or mark taken right from the notification.

💊

Medication Management

Add medications manually or scan a label with AI. Track dosing schedules, refill quantities, supply levels, and discontinuation history.

📈

Health Tracking

Log vitals (blood pressure, glucose, weight, heart rate), journal entries with mood, symptoms, and notes. Spot trends with visual charts.

👥

Care Network

Add providers by specialty, invite caregivers with role-based access, and manage emergency contacts with a shareable ICE card and QR code.

🤖

AI Assistant

Ask about interactions, side effects, or schedules. Scan prescription labels with your camera. All AI is opt-in, consent-gated, and runs server-side.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

No app store download. No insurance forms. Works on any device.

Create your account

Sign up with email or Google. Accept the beta terms, choose your AI preferences, and add MarinLoop to your home screen.

Add your medications

Enter medications manually or scan a prescription label. Set doses, times, quantities, and reminder preferences.

Build your daily routine

Log doses on the timeline, track vitals and journal entries, invite caregivers, and let push reminders keep you on schedule.

Trust & Safety

Built with clear boundaries.

We tell you exactly what MarinLoop is — and what it isn't.

✅

What MarinLoop Is

A personal medication-management workflow for reminders, adherence tracking, vitals logging, and caregiver coordination.

❌

What It Is Not

Not a diagnostic tool, not a clinician substitute, not an emergency alerting system, and not a HIPAA deployment for covered-entity workflows in this beta.

🤖

Optional AI

AI features are opt-in and revocable. Core medication tracking, reminders, and adherence work without AI enabled.

🔒

Row-Level Security

Your data is isolated at the database level with Supabase RLS. No other user can access your records.

📦

Data Portability

Export all your health data as JSON or delete your account and data entirely — anytime, from your profile.

📜

In-App Legal

Terms of service, privacy policy, and trust center are accessible inside the app with consent gating on first use.

Under the Hood

Built for trust, not just features.

The details that matter when health data is involved.

📱

Installable PWA

Add to your home screen on any device. Works offline with local action queuing and automatic sync when reconnected. No app store required.

🐒

Drug Safety Lookups

Real-time interaction checks and allergy alerts via NIH RxNav and OpenFDA. No personal data is transmitted to these services.

🔐

Privacy by Design

HTTPS-only transport, encryption at rest, Sentry error reporting with PII scrubbing, and AI keys that never touch the client.
