Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built for patients managing daily medications and caregivers who need visibility without complexity.

📅

Daily Timeline

See your full day at a glance — medications, vitals, and notes organized by time. Tap to log, swipe to reschedule.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Push notifications, snooze support, and escalating alerts. Never miss a dose even on your busiest days.

👥

Care Network

Invite family members or caregivers to view adherence. They see status updates — you keep full control.

❤

Vitals Tracking

Log blood pressure, glucose, weight, and more. Spot trends over time with simple visual charts.

📝

Health Journal

Quick daily notes on symptoms, mood, and side effects. Export anytime for doctor appointments.

📱

Installable PWA

Add to your home screen on any device. Works offline, syncs when connected. No app store required.

Trust & Safety

Built with clear boundaries.

We tell you exactly what MarinLoop is — and what it isn't.

✅

What MarinLoop Is

A personal medication-management workflow for reminders, adherence tracking, vitals logging, and caregiver coordination.

❌

What It Is Not

Not a diagnostic tool, not a clinician substitute, not an emergency alert system, and not a HIPAA deployment for covered entities in this beta.

🤖

Optional AI

AI features are fully opt-in and revocable. Core medication tracking works perfectly without AI enabled.

🔒

Row-Level Security

Your data is isolated at the database level. No other user can access your records, period.

📦

Data Portability

Export all your data or delete your account at any time. Your data, your choice.

📜

In-App Legal

Terms of service and privacy policy accessible inside the app, with consent gating on first use.
